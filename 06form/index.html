WELCOME!

Here you'll find a small compendium of the most important basic lingo for getting started in the FGC. We won't delve too deeply into the finer details and more obscure terms, but we hope to provide a good starter guide for everyone. For a much more in-depth resource with anything and everything contained within, visit the Fighting Game Glossary by Infilament.
